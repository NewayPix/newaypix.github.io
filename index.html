Who we are

NewayPix Entertainment is a technology company focused on the development of digital games. Founded in 2013, NewayPix Entertainment sets itself the ideal of exacerbating dreams. "We all have the ability to dream" and through games, NewayPix Entertainment wants to give back to people this incredible superpower that we all have, creativity!

All members of NewayPix Entertainment believe on the inspirational potential that dreams can provide and therefore invite you to join our dream. Allow yourself to live each of our worlds, every bit of it is part of the NewayPix dream.
